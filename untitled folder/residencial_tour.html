Residencial Tour

My name is Lucas and I am truly in love with Cascais! In search for the so dreamed quality of life, I moved with my family from Rio de Janeiro to Cascais and the Village enchanted me so much that I decided to show its charms to everyone! But Cascais goes beyond the charming alleys of the famous Historic Center, being a large municipality with several areas and housing options for all tastes and budgets. When I moved here, I felt myself with lack of knowledge and information, which created fear and insecurity when choosing where to live with my family. With that in mind, I took everything I learned since then and created a Residential Tour. Based on the profile of each family, I develop a personalized itinerary and show the main residential areas, price ranges, schools, health centers, markets, local commerce, transport, negative and positive points of each one of those zones.

Duration: 8 hours – Price: €250 – up to 6 pax
